# Facimus Curiositatem

**"We do it for the curiosity."**

Welcome to **Facimus Curiositatem**, a collaborative space for a small team of friends dedicated to exploring the "how" and "why" of technology. We don't limit ourselves to one niche; if it involves code or components, we’re interested.

## 🛠 What We Do
We build small-scale, experimental projects across various domains:
*   **Software Development:** Web apps, scripts, and utility tools.
*   **Hardware & IoT:** Prototyping with microcontrollers and physical computing.
*   **Research & Exploration:** Diving into new frameworks and legacy tech alike.

## 🚀 Our Philosophy
This organization serves as our collective **portfolio and playground**. Every repository here is a byproduct of our genuine curiosity and our desire to learn by doing.
